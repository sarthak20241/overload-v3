Overload

Delete your account

For the Overload mobile app (iOS & Android) by Sarthak Kumar.

Fastest path: open the app → Profile tab → scroll to Delete Account. One confirmation tap wipes your workouts, routines, body stats, AI Coach history, and deletes your sign-in. There is no undo.

If you can't open the app

If you've uninstalled Overload or can't sign in, email support@tryoverload.app from the address on your account. Subject line: Delete my Overload account. Include the email you used to sign in. We'll confirm deletion within 7 business days.

What we delete

When you request deletion (in-app or by email), we permanently remove:

  • Your Clerk sign-in record (email + linked Apple/Google identifiers).
  • All workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights, durations, and notes you logged.
  • All routines you built.
  • All body stats you entered (weight history, body-fat history, goal weight, units).
  • All AI Coach conversations and prompt history.
  • Your XP, streaks, and level progression.
  • Any bug reports you submitted via Profile → Send Bug Report.

What we keep, and for how long

For security and abuse-prevention purposes we retain minimal, non-identifying records of the deletion event itself (an opaque user-id hash + timestamp) for up to 30 days. After 30 days that record is purged.

Server logs (HTTPS request lines, anonymous error stack traces) may persist for up to 90 days in our hosting providers' standard log retention before automatic rotation. Those logs do not contain workout content.

Reinstating an account

Deletion is irreversible. If you change your mind, signing up again with the same email gives you a fresh account with no historical data.
