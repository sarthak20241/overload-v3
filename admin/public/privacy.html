Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 May 2026 · Last updated: 25 May 2026

This policy explains what data the Overload mobile app (the "App") collects, why, and how you control it. Overload is a personal workout tracker — the data we handle is the data you choose to log.

Plain summary. Overload stores your account (email), workouts, routines, body stats, and AI Coach conversations. We do not sell your data. We do not run third-party advertising or analytics trackers. You can delete your account at any time from inside the app — it wipes your data.

2. What we collect

Account data

  • Email address and authentication identifiers (via Clerk, our auth provider).
  • If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the identifiers those providers return — typically email + a stable user ID. We never receive your password.

Fitness data you log

  • Workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weight, duration, notes.
  • Routines you create.
  • Body stats you choose to enter (e.g. weight, goal weight, unit preference).

AI Coach interactions

  • The messages you send to the AI Coach and the responses returned.
  • Coach prompts include a summary of your recent workouts so it can answer in context.

Device + diagnostic data

  • App version, OS version, device model — used to investigate crashes and bugs.
  • Bug reports you submit voluntarily through the Profile screen.

We do not collect: location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, advertising IDs, or health-app data.

3. How we use it

  • To provide the app's features (track workouts, render analytics, answer Coach questions).
  • To keep you signed in across devices.
  • To investigate and fix bugs you report or that crash the app.
  • To improve the product over time.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Who we share it with

We use the following processors. They handle data on our behalf under their own privacy commitments:

  • Clerk — authentication (privacy policy).
  • Supabase — database + storage (privacy policy).
  • Anthropic — AI Coach inference, when you use the Coach (privacy policy). Anthropic does not train on customer API content.
  • Voyage AI — embeddings used by the Coach for context retrieval (privacy policy).
  • Expo (EAS) — over-the-air updates + crash diagnostics (privacy policy).
  • RevenueCat — subscription + in-app purchase management, when you buy a paid plan (privacy policy). Receives your account identifier to attribute purchases to your account.
  • Apple / Google — sign-in, app distribution, and payment processing for paid plans.

We do not share or sell your data for advertising.

5. Where data is stored

Account + fitness data is stored in Supabase Postgres (region [PLACEHOLDER: e.g. us-east-1]). Auth state is stored in Clerk. AI Coach inference runs through Anthropic's API.

6. How long we keep it

  • While your account exists, we keep your data.
  • When you delete your account from inside the app (Profile → Delete Account), we wipe your Supabase rows and ask Clerk to delete your account.
  • Backups may persist briefly (up to 30 days) until they roll off.

7. Your rights

You can:

  • Access — see your data inside the app at any time.
  • Correct — edit any of it inside the app.
  • Delete — Profile → Delete Account permanently removes your data.
  • Export — email [PLACEHOLDER: support@yourdomain.com] and we will send your data within 30 days.
  • Object / Restrict — email us; if you're in the EU/UK/California you have additional rights under GDPR / CCPA which we honor.

8. Security

Tokens are stored in the device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). All network traffic uses HTTPS. Supabase Row-Level-Security policies scope every row to your user ID so no other user can read your data. We are a small team — no system is perfect — and we will notify affected users in the event of a breach.

9. Children

Overload is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has signed up, email us and we will delete the account.

10. Changes

If we materially change this policy we will update the "Last updated" date and notify users inside the app on next launch. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
